My Rules for Acquiring Information

  • If the material is well packaged, easy to read but has a price (e.g. books, paid video channels, subscription newsletters), pay for it;
  • If the material is free, difficult to read but has gems in it (e.g. academic papers, raw text/video feed, obscure ancient classics), power through it
  • If the material is well packaged, easy to read AND free (e.g. online magazines, opinion columns, sensational lecture talks with well dressed, well spoken speakers), be very sceptical, because those who are willing go through the trouble of making things interesting and give them out for free often have an ulterior motive.
